Devconnect in Argentina needed an event that could rise to the occasion and offer every tool to arrive in November well prepared. The Aleph Festival was born from that need: three months of pop-up city, from August 25 to November 22, 2025, the fourth edition of Aleph, once again with the Aleph Hub as its home. The intention was for Buenos Aires to work for over two months as the center of activity of the crypto world, and for those who had already decided to come in November to move their flights up.

The festival was designed to support builders at whatever stage they were in, as the frame for Crecimiento's Builder Funnel: one program for each stage. The Aleph Hackathon, from August 29 to 31, for those starting from an idea. The Buildathon Season, with acceleration programs co-built with protocols across verticals like Consumer, Privacy, AI, SocialFi and DeFi. And the Startup World Cup, with its grand final at Devconnect, for startups ready to compete. Founder School and the Crecimiento Support Program's initiatives joined that path.

![Aleph Festival brings together everything this ecosystem needs to grow](/images/blog/aleph-festival-1.jpg)

The festival was much more than the funnel. The community organized events every single day: meetups, workshops, bootcamps, summits and roundtables, along with Crecimiento classics like [Regulation Day](/blog/regulation-day-en), which held its edition at Devconnect. The experience was completed with wellness and culture tracks: run clubs, yoga at the Hub and cultural activities.

The Aleph Festival was also the main platform for the Bring Argentina Onchain campaign and the final stretch towards Devconnect Buenos Aires.
