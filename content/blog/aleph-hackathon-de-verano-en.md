The second edition of the Aleph Hackathon ran from December 12 to 15, 2024, within Aleph de Verano. It was the first in a hybrid format: four days of building between Aleph Hub and the rest of the world, with 280 builders from 30 countries.

![Builders at work during the Aleph Hackathon de Verano](/images/blog/hackathon-verano-1.jpg)

Prizes exceeded 70 thousand dollars, backed by Optimism, Zama and ZKsync. The preparation included five workshops, and the teams worked alongside a panel of 22 judges, mentors and speakers, among them Camila Russo (The Defiant), Ana Belén (Arbitrum Foundation), Kun Peng (Stanford) and Skylar Weaver (Ethereum Foundation).

![Hands on: an FHE project taking shape](/images/blog/hackathon-verano-2.jpg)

The winners were Adversarium, Superswap and Underloans, with a special mention for OptimismPay.

The pop-up city where it happened has its own story: [Aleph de Verano](/blog/aleph-de-verano-december-2024).
