The Aleph Hub was selected to host the Buenos Aires Ethereum Community Hub, the first official Ethereum Community Hub in Latin America, backed by the Ethereum Foundation's Ethereum Everywhere team. It works as a permanent space for builders working on Ethereum.

[video](https://youtu.be/2KQST3ZkiOQ)

The Hub runs in two formats. The monthly meetups gather builders, researchers, investors and enthusiasts. Each edition goes deep into a specific topic, with the participation of Ethereum Foundation researchers and leading projects of the ecosystem. The Ethereum Coworking Days, in turn, transform the Aleph Hub once a week into a workspace dedicated to Buenos Aires' Ethereum community: a curated coworking where selected builders, founders and developers work side by side. Access is application-based with limited spots; applications remain open on a rolling basis and, once accepted, each participant can attend every Coworking Day of that month.

The Hub positions Argentina as a node of the global Ethereum ecosystem and anchors the Foundation's institutional presence in the region, with Crecimiento as the local actor hosting and operating it.
