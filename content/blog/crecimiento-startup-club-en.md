The Crecimiento Startup Club was a long-term Crecimiento membership for founders building in Web3, artificial intelligence and frontier tech, and it ran throughout 2025. The program started from the vision that supporting a startup should not end with a program lasting a few weeks; it had to be constant. Founders need continuity, visibility, community and a network of trust to keep growing, and the Club existed to sustain that bond over time.

The membership was free for selected startups, with no fees and no expiration date, and it was open both to those who had already gone through ecosystem programs and to those joining for the first time. What was asked in return was to amplify the announcements of other startups in the ecosystem and to support one another.

Members entered the Crecimiento Startup Directory and carried the title of "Crecimiento Startup", a signal of quality, vision and trust before partners, users and investors. They gained visibility across the movement's channels, a guaranteed place at the Aleph Hub with its weekly events and founder meetups, early access to Crecimiento programs, and a network of mentors, investors and experts, with weekly mentorship hours and a private group among the selected founders.

The Club launched in July 2025, in the middle of Season 4, and remained active all year, keeping startups tied to the movement for the long term. 150 startups came through the Club.
