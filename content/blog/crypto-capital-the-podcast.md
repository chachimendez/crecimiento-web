Crypto Capital is Crecimiento's podcast: conversations with leading figures of the crypto ecosystem about their ideas, their projects and their vision of the future. Its first season, three episodes long, was recorded live during Aleph de Verano, in December 2024.

The first episode welcomed Vitalik Buterin, co-founder of Ethereum, to talk about decentralization, blockchain governance and the growth of Argentina's crypto ecosystem. The second, Eric Hill, a Web3 public policy expert, on Argentina's regulatory challenges and the potential of sandboxes and special economic zones. And the third, Juan Benet, founder of Protocol Labs and one of Crecimiento's co-founders, on Argentina's role in the future of crypto and the origins of the movement.
