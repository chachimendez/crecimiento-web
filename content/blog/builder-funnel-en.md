In 2026, Crecimiento deepens its long-term commitment to builders, founders, and startups through a renewed Builder Funnel: a structured pathway designed to support teams from the earliest stage of an idea through growth and global projection.

The Builder Funnel reflects how Crecimiento approaches ecosystem-building: progress accelerates when builders and founders have continuity, the right environment, and a system that helps projects evolve over time. This funnel brings that structure together by combining talent, mentorship, community, and real-world execution into a coherent journey, organized around three interconnected stages:

- [Aleph Hackathon](/blog/aleph-hackathon-march-26-en): the entry point for builders and early-stage teams.
- [Acceleration Season](/blog/uniswap-acceleration-season-en): dedicated to iteration, consolidation, and measurable progress.
- [Startup World Cup](/blog/startup-world-cup-en): focused on visibility, validation, and global exposure.

The Builder Funnel is built around continuity. As projects move forward, teams gain access to broader networks, deeper support, stronger mentorship, and increasing levels of execution and exposure. Crecimiento's commitment is to empower builders, founders, and startups with the environment, support, and network they need to build with ambition, consistency, and long-term vision.
