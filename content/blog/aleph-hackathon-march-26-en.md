The fifth edition of the Aleph Hackathon ran within Aleph March '26 and gathered more than 800 participants and 142 projects, with 12 chapters across five countries: Buenos Aires, San Juan, Salta, Rosario, La Plata and Mar del Plata in Argentina; Cochabamba, La Paz and Santa Cruz in Bolivia; Lima in Peru; Cuenca in Ecuador; and Cartago in Costa Rica.

![The track presentation before a full house](/images/blog/hackathon-march-26-1.jpg)

The focus was on the tracks: the edition expanded its scope to four verticals, crypto, artificial intelligence, biotech and robotics, and brought partners LucAI and Y-Hat into the Biotech Track. The preparation included more than six workshops.

![The Robotics Track left its creatures too](/images/blog/hackathon-march-26-2.jpg)

Each track had its winner:

- Avalanche Track: AutoBounty
- Fiserv Track: Clever
- GenLayer Track: TreasuryPilot
- Biotech Track: Consient
- Robotics Track: AlephRob
- Best Project PL Genesis: AutoBounty

AutoBounty took a double win: its track and the PL Genesis best project award.

![Work tables during the hacking hours](/images/blog/hackathon-march-26-3.jpg)

The hackathon was the starting point of Season 5's Builder Funnel, which continues with the Acceleration Season and the Startup World Cup.

![Between hacking sessions there was time for the house T-Rex](/images/blog/hackathon-march-26-4.jpg)
