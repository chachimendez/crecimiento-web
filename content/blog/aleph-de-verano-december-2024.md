From December 9 to 20, 2024, Aleph de Verano was Crecimiento's second pop-up city: two weeks to close the year building, celebrate what had been done and leave everything ready for 2025. More than 1,500 people took part.

[video](https://youtu.be/5eF0V552m58?list=PL0Jb2_9xzReITJFE5shjRa7kp6IxOyjwO)

The programming combined thematic days on DeFi, infrastructure, governance and AI with a four-day hybrid hackathon with 280 builders, a three-day FHE and zero-knowledge bootcamp with 65 students, a workshop edition of Regulation Day alongside the Cámara Argentina Fintech, a wellness track and 15 side events organized by the community. More than 50 partners and sponsors came along, and over 100 speakers from all over the world took the stage.

![Vitalik Buterin at a talk during Aleph de Verano](/images/blog/aleph-de-verano-1.jpg)

Guests included Vitalik Buterin, Juan Benet and representatives from the Ethereum Foundation, Zama, ZKsync, Optimism, Aztec, Starknet and Avalanche.

![The pop-up city welcomed visitors of all kinds](/images/blog/aleph-de-verano-3.jpg)

Aleph de Verano was as much an event as a thank-you: a chance to reconnect the community that had made the year possible, reflect on what had been built, and start shaping what was coming.
